Hostel Overview

The hostel facility at Government Polytechnic Atmakur provides accommodation for students from different regions. The hostel ensures a safe, disciplined, and comfortable environment for students to focus on their studies.

Hostel Facilities

  • Spacious student rooms
  • 24/7 water facility
  • Mess facility for food
  • Security and supervision
  • Clean drinking water

Hostel Rules

  • Students must maintain discipline inside the hostel.
  • Hostel timing must be strictly followed.
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must keep their rooms clean.
  • Visitors are allowed only during permitted hours.

Mess Timings

  • Breakfast: 8:30 AM – 9:30 AM
  • Lunch: 01:20 PM – 2:00 PM
  • Dinner: 8:00 PM – 9:30 PM
